SaaS Products Showcasing

The SaaS model is a new ray of hope for SME entrepreneurs. Digitization gives entrepreneurs the opportunity to reap the financial benefits of low capital investment. BASIS has taken the initiative to develop the SaaS model among local SME entrepreneurs. Using the SaaS model, SME entrepreneurs can access the software for free for a month by visiting the link https://saas.basis.org.bd/
